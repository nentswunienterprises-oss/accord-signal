Accord Signal OS

Clarify. Signal. Align. Condition. Verify.

Accord Signal OS is the operating system for aligning attention, building capability, and strengthening roles inside an organization.

Phase 01

Clarify

Define the role, responsibility, success standard, decision boundaries, and accountability lines.

Phase 02

Signal

Design dashboards, briefings, checklists, meeting rhythm, and feedback structures that guide attention.

Phase 03

Align

Direct attention toward the right priorities, behaviors, standards, and weekly points of execution.

Phase 04

Condition

Build capability through repetition, assignment, correction, pressure, feedback, and progression.

Phase 05

Verify

Assess readiness and determine whether the person or team can carry weight reliably without rescue.

The Three Maps

The operating system relies on three internal maps.

Role Map

Purpose, responsibilities, success outcomes, decision rights, required capabilities, and accountability relationships.

Signal Map

Instructions, dashboards, meetings, manager feedback, weekly priorities, performance measures, and communication channels.

Capability Map

Skills, behaviors, judgment, communication, execution habits, pressure stability, and leadership readiness.

Readiness Levels

People move through stages from unclear to signal carrier.

Level 1: Unclear

The role, standard, or priority is not yet visible enough to carry reliably.

Level 2: Oriented

The role is understood, but repetition and guidance are still required.

Level 3: Conditioning

The person is actively practicing the required behavior and receiving correction.

Level 4: Stabilizing

Performance is becoming reliable in normal conditions but still needs pressure testing.

Level 5: Ready

The role can place weight on the person with confidence, consistency, and accountability.

Level 6: Signal Carrier

The person can now transmit clarity, reinforce standards, and help shape others.
