THE ACCORD SIGNAL CONSTITUTION
VERSION 1

PREAMBLE
Accord Signal exists to develop capability through conditioning.
We believe institutions rise and fall according to the quality of the people who carry them.
Ideas alone do not create outcomes.
Systems alone do not create outcomes.
People create outcomes.
Yet capability does not emerge automatically.
Capability must be conditioned.
Accord Signal exists to design the infrastructures, pathways, systems, and environments through which reliable capability is developed.
We do not manage people.
We condition capability.

ARTICLE I
THE NATURE OF CAPABILITY
Capability is the ability to produce reliable outcomes under real-world conditions.
Knowledge is not capability.
Potential is not capability.
Ambition is not capability.
Interest is not capability.
Capability is demonstrated through consistent performance.
The purpose of Accord Signal is to increase the reliability of human performance.

ARTICLE II
THE ATTENTION PRINCIPLE
Capability follows attention.
What receives sustained attention develops.
What receives inconsistent attention deteriorates.
Human development is fundamentally an attention problem before it becomes a performance problem.
Accord Signal therefore designs infrastructures that direct attention toward what matters.

ARTICLE III
THE CONDITIONING PRINCIPLE
Capability is conditioned.
It is not wished into existence.
It is not motivated into existence.
It is not inspired into existence.
Capability emerges through:
repetition
exposure
correction
accountability
consequence
evidence
Conditioning transforms potential into performance.

ARTICLE IV
THE SIGNAL PRINCIPLE
Every environment sends signals.
Signals influence attention.
Attention influences behavior.
Behavior influences outcomes.
Organizations often fail because their signals contradict their objectives.
Accord Signal exists to align signals with desired outcomes.
The purpose of infrastructure is not merely administration.
The purpose of infrastructure is direction.

ARTICLE V
THE ROLE PRINCIPLE
Every institution is carried by roles.
Roles are not job titles.
Roles are responsibilities.
A role exists to produce outcomes.
Accord Signal develops role architectures that clarify:
expectations
responsibilities
standards
progression
accountability
Ambiguity weakens institutions.
Clarity strengthens them.

ARTICLE VI
THE CERTIFICATION PRINCIPLE
Capability must be demonstrated.
Claims are insufficient.
Intentions are insufficient.
Participation is insufficient.
Certification exists to verify performance.
A certified individual has demonstrated capability according to defined standards.
Certification is evidence.

ARTICLE VII
THE READINESS PRINCIPLE
Institutions require readiness before responsibility.
Responsibility should not be assigned merely because an individual desires it.
Responsibility should be assigned when readiness has been demonstrated.
Readiness protects individuals.
Readiness protects institutions.
Readiness protects outcomes.

ARTICLE VIII
THE INFRASTRUCTURE PRINCIPLE
Infrastructure shapes behavior.
People respond to environments.
Environments influence decisions.
Institutions therefore require intentional infrastructures.
Accord Signal designs environments that reinforce capability rather than undermine it.
The objective is not control.
The objective is alignment.

ARTICLE IX
THE DISCIPLINE PRINCIPLE
Undisciplined systems create undisciplined outcomes.
Undisciplined attention creates undisciplined behavior.
Undisciplined behavior creates institutional weakness.
Accord Signal identifies and removes structural sources of drift, distraction, inconsistency, and misalignment.
The goal is not punishment.
The goal is reliability.

ARTICLE X
THE STANDARD
Accord Signal does not optimize for comfort.
Accord Signal does not optimize for appearances.
Accord Signal does not optimize for participation alone.
Accord Signal optimizes for capability.
The measure of success is not activity.
The measure of success is demonstrated readiness and reliable performance.

DECLARATION
We believe capability can be conditioned.
We believe attention determines development.
We believe environments influence behavior.
We believe readiness should precede responsibility.
We believe institutions require capable people.
We exist to design the infrastructures through which capability becomes reliable.
This is the purpose of Accord Signal.
